Progress in Peace takes part in a workshop focused on the Yazidi Survivors Law

In pursuit of its objective to ensure that survivors of the ISIL conflict in Iraq can access their entitlement to reparations, Mr. Farhad Barakat, Deputy Executive Director, engaged in an intensive three-day training workshop held on the 24th, 25th, and 26th of September. This training took place in the city of Duhok and was conducted by a team from CVT organization, in collaboration with the Coalition for Just Reparation(C4JR).
